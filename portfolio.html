WHAT IS DIGITAL MARKETING

Digital marketing is a comprehensive strategy encompassing various online channels and techniques aimed at promoting products, services, or brands in the digital realm. It includes activities such as search engine optimization (SEO), social media marketing, email marketing, content creation, paid advertising, and website optimization. The core objective of digital marketing is to connect with target audiences, drive engagement, generate leads, and ultimately, boost conversions and sales in a highly measurable and data-driven manner. It leverages the vast reach of the internet to create meaningful connections with potential customers and foster brand awareness, making it an indispensable tool for businesses looking to thrive in the digital age.
